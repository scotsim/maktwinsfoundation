Makinde Twins Foundation: Empowering Education for Every Child

Makinde Twins Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to ensuring every child has the right to remain in school, regardless of financial constraints. We provide educational funding, resources, and support to empower students at all levels.

Our Foundation Programs

SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAMS

Providing financial support and scholarships to ensure children can continue their education without interruption.

EDUCATIONAL RESOURCES

Distributing textbooks, school supplies, and learning materials to students in need across communities.

TUTORING & MENTORSHIP

Connecting students with qualified tutors and mentors to improve academic performance and build confidence.

Workshops & Breakout Sessions

GLOBAL PERSPECTIVES

Exposure to global trends and ideas to help youth navigate and thrive in a changing world.

PERSONAL DEVELOPMENT

Sessions focused on mental health, self-awareness, and building resilience for a fulfilling life.
